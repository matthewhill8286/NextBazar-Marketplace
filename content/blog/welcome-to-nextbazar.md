## A Marketplace Built for Cyprus

After months of building, testing, and refining, NextBazar is here. We set out to create the marketplace Cyprus deserves — one that's fast, smart, and puts trust first.

Whether you're looking for a car in Limassol, an apartment in Nicosia, or a vintage watch in Paphos, NextBazar makes it easy to find exactly what you need.

## What Makes NextBazar Different

**AI-Powered Search** — Our search doesn't just match keywords. It understands what you're looking for and surfaces the most relevant results, even if you don't use the exact right words.

**Instant Messaging** — No more waiting for email replies. Message any seller in real-time, right from the listing page. Get answers fast and close deals faster.

**Trusted Sellers** — Our Pro and Business seller plans mean verified shops with branded pages, analytics, and a commitment to quality. Look for the verified badge.

**Three Languages** — NextBazar is fully available in English, Greek, and Russian, reflecting the communities that make Cyprus so vibrant.

## For Sellers

We've built powerful tools for sellers at every level:

- **Starter (Free)** — Up to 5 listings. Perfect for occasional sellers.
- **Pro (€29/mo)** — Up to 50 listings with analytics, priority support, and your own shop page.
- **Business (€89/mo)** — Unlimited listings, CSV import, AI descriptions, and everything a dealer needs.

## What's Coming Next

We're just getting started. Here's a preview of what we're working on:

- **Buyer+ Plans** — Price drop alerts, priority access to new listings, and more.
- **Mobile App** — A native iOS and Android experience is in the works.
- **Expanded Categories** — More specialised categories for boats, services, and jobs.
